Identified problem

The busy lives of modern people who no longer have time to invest time in their hobbies.

Our solution

A mobile application that allows searching nearby or in a desired location for existing sports or recreational opportunities. With this easy to use application, you have at hand sports recommendations based on interests and location, sports equipment rentals and land reservations. Side-note: With this application we also want to remove the anonymity of some sports not so practiced in Romania or even of some halls and lands not so well known, maybe even from your proximity!

Why us?

  1. online booking services
  2. visibility for unknown gyms
  3. healthy lifestyle
  4. search based on interests (equipment, sports)
  5. search based on location (vacation, your neighborhood)
  6. fixed commission for confirmed and unconfirmed reservations

Do you ...?
