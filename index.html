Our solution

A mobile application that allows searching nearby or in a desired location for existing sports or recreational opportunities. With this easy to use application, you have at hand:

sports recommendations based on interests and location
sports equipment rentals and land reservations.

Side-note: With this application we also want to remove the anonymity of some sports not so practiced in Romania or even of some halls and lands not so well known, maybe even from your proximity!
